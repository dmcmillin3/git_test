Project02 - Conversion Machine

Volume Converter

Have you ever been in the midst of preparing a fresh batch of cookies, or some other delicious baked treat, when suddenly you realize you've made a great mistake... You forgot to get measuring cups and all you have is a teaspoon to measure out all of your ingredients! Well, there is no need to fear. This conversion program is the solution for you! Just enter in the amount of cups that you need, and the program will give you the same amount in teaspoons! Give it a try!
