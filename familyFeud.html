Rules

The game is played through levels. There will be a given number of possible answers, if your answer is not one of the most popular then that counts as a strike, 3 strikes and you are out. You enter your ONE WORD answers into each given space, after click the "check" button. You will then either be able to move on the the next level or you loose the game. Good Luck!
